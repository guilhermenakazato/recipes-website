You'll need:

  • 1 (14oz) can sweet condensed milk
  • 4 Tbsp cocoa powder, sifted
  • 2 Tbsp butter, plus more for rolling balls
  • A pinch of salt
  • Good quality chocolate sprinkles (or any other type of sprinkles you like)
